Site Engineering Assistant

A comprehensive field reference for civil engineers and surveyors. Designed for fast, offline access to:

  • Total Station (TS16) field procedures
  • Leica GPS / RTK workflows
  • DWG → DXF preparation checklists
  • Expanded terminology: tunnels, highways, RC frames, railways
  • Problem‑solving scenarios & QA checklists
  • Interactive calculators (slope, volume, manhole rings...)

How to use:
• Tap any bottom tab to switch modules.
• Use the search (🔍) to find terms across all modules.
• In the Site Support module, use the category tabs to filter terminology.
• Tap the 🧮 button for quick field calculators.
• Toggle dark/light theme with the switch.
• The ↑ button appears when you scroll – tap to return to top.
